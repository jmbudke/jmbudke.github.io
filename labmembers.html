Interested in joining the lab?
We are activiely recruiting UTK undergraduate students to join the lab to work on research projects focusing on moss parent-offspring conflict. This includes the potential for skill bulding in histology, morphological data collection, molecular systematics, and fieldwork. If our lab seems like it might be a good fit for you, please send an email expressing your interest with your CV/resume and unofficial transcripts attached to Dr. Jessica Budke jbudke@utk.edu

Unfortunately we are not recruiting graduate students for the December 2023 application deadline.
Please check back for open positions in fall 2024.
